Why This Matters?

The CardioX Insights Dashboard was developed to demystify complex medical datasets, making cardiovascular risk factors transparent and accessible through powerful visualization.

With heart disease remaining one of the leading global health challenges, identifying the contributing variables is paramount. This interactive tool brings attention to deep-seated patterns surrounding:

  • Age Distribution
  • Gender Disparities
  • Blood Pressure
  • Cholesterol Impact
  • Diabetic Complications
